About

RAIDS Lab explores robust machine learning systems, trustworthy data pipelines, and security-aware AI deployment. We build methods that are practical, reproducible, and impactful for real-world systems.

Our team collaborates across machine learning, systems, and human-centered computing to design end-to-end AI solutions that remain reliable under distribution shift, adversarial conditions, and resource constraints.

Research Areas

Trustworthy Foundation Models

Alignment, uncertainty estimation, red teaming, and safety evaluation.

Data-Centric AI

Data quality diagnosis, efficient labeling, and dataset lifecycle tooling.

AI Systems and Infrastructure

Inference optimization, scalable serving, and deployment observability.

Security and Privacy

Adversarial robustness, model hardening, and privacy-preserving learning.
