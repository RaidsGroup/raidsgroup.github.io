Demos

Robot and System Demonstrations

Task-level demonstrations from RAIDS research programs—covering embodied data acquisition, aerial dexterous manipulation, and human-to-robot skill transfer. All videos play inline; no external links required.

TeleX

  • Universal compatibility
  • Comfortable wear
  • Exceptional dexterity

A versatile industrial embodied-intelligence data acquisition terminal for vision–tactile multimodal capture. It integrates readily with diverse general-purpose robotic arms and dexterous hands, and supports egocentric data collection.

Aerial Dexterous Manipulation

  • Embodied Platform
  • Real-2-Sim-2-Real

A novel aerial embodied-intelligence platform for dexterous manipulation and interaction in 3D space. It combines aerial mobility with robotic arm manipulation, supports embodied task learning in both simulation and real-world environments, and enables a Real-2-Sim-2-Real workflow for developing transferable aerial manipulation policies.

Human-to-Robot Skill Transfer

  • MR teleoperation
  • Bimanual dexterity
  • Tactile imitation

A human-to-robot skill transfer framework for bimanual dexterous manipulation. MR-assisted teleoperation enables intuitive human demonstrations, while force/tactile-based imitation learning helps the robot acquire and reproduce complex manipulation skills.
