Research

Core Research Directions

We study embodied intelligence, physical reasoning, data-centric learning, and secure AI systems.

Embodied Intelligence

Skill learning, manipulation policies, and long-horizon agent planning.

Physical Reasoning

Object dynamics prediction, contact reasoning, and interactive world models.

Data-Centric AI

Data construction, benchmark design, and evaluation under distribution shifts.

Secure and Reliable Systems

Reliable deployment, robustness diagnostics, and safety-aware system design.

Current Focus Topics

  • Vision-Language-Action model efficiency and deployment.
  • Interactive physical reasoning for robotics.
  • Failure analysis and correction in embodied tasks.
  • Scalable evaluation datasets for general-purpose agents.
